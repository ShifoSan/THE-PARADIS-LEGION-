3. The Legion's Master Guide to Prestige

Prestige is at the core of AoTR player growth. Maximizing your Prestige gains unlocks powerful abilities and level caps.

  • The Path to Prestige: How to Prestige: Requirement: To prestige, you only need to reach the maximum level for your current prestige rank. Prestige Scrolls: You do NOT need a prestige scroll to prestige. Prestige scrolls are a separate item used only for rerolling memories.
  • The Aftermath: Consequences & Rewards: Prestiging is a monumental step that resets certain aspects of your progress while granting permanent buffs. What You KEEP After Prestiging: Your Titan Serum. Your current in-game Streak. The Best Boost you have selected (e.g., Luck). What RESETS After Prestiging: Your Level resets to 1. Your Skill Tree resets. Your ODM Grade resets. The Permanent Buffs You GAIN: New Memory Slot: You gain an additional passive/permanent memory slot with every prestige. Choice of Permanent Boost: You can choose one of the following permanent stat increases: +7.5% Luck OR +15% EXP OR +15% Gold Increased Gem Payouts: Every prestige adds a permanent +10 gems to your mission and raid payouts. Increased Legendary Chance: You gain a permanent +0.005% chance to roll legendary families per prestige. Increased Level Cap: Your maximum attainable level increases with each prestige: Prestige 1: New Level Cap is 125 Prestige 2: New Level Cap is 150 Prestige 3: New Level Cap is 175 Prestige 4: New Level Cap is 200
  • Converting Your Fortune: Gold-to-Gem Conversion: Upon prestiging, all of your accumulated Gold is automatically converted into Gems. The conversion rate improves as you reach higher prestige levels. Prestiging to P1: 1500 Gold = 1 Gem. Prestiging to P2: 1250 Gold = 1 Gem. Prestiging to P3: 1000 Gold = 1 Gem. Prestiging to P4: 1000 Gold = 1 Gem. Example Conversion (with 500 Million Gold which is the cap on gold-gem conversion): Prestige 1: 333,333 gems. Prestige 2: 400,000 gems. Prestige 3: 500,000 gems. Prestige 4: 500,000 gems.

Mastering Prestige timing and buff management will give you an edge in rank and combat effectiveness.

AoTR FAQs

What are all the secret achievements?

Omnipotent - Have all achievements (beside Omnipotent)

Overlord - 50,000 kills

Fearless - 20,000 streak

Champion - Top 3 on monthly leaderboard (for the end of the month)

Immortalized - 500 deaths

Warbringer - Complete any Aberrant raid with the first 8 modifiers

NOTE: You must get at least 25.0% damage credit to receive Warbringer

Legend - 2,500 missions

Encounter - Play a game with a Developer, Administrator, or Moderator

NOTE: Trello Staff, Trial Moderators, Helpers, and Testers can NOT give Encounter

How should I upgrade my gear?

The most important stat to upgrade is Damage, as being able to 1-shot titans will both make the game much easier and much faster. Upgrading all stats is important, though, as your overall Grade is based on the average of all of your stats' Grades combined, but the upgrade cost is only based on the Grade of that specific stat. Increasing your overall Grade as quickly and cheaply as possible will let you enter higher difficulties, and thus earn better rewards.

How many achievements can I skip for Shiki?

One. Make it count.

This includes secret (red) achievements. For most players, Champion is the best achievement to skip.

When is X2 Family?

Starts every weekend on:

Saturday 12:00 A.M. (UTC) - Tuesday 12:00 A.M. (UTC)

Friday 7:00 P.M. (EST) - Monday 7:00 P.M. (EST)

Saturday 12:00 A.M. (UK) - Tuesday 12:00 A.M. (UK)

The game runs on UTC timezone, just convert to your own timezone if it isn't listed.

How do Streak Gems work?

On Hard and above, every time you complete a mission where your total streak hit a new multiple of 50, you get Gems based on the mission's difficulty, as well as your Streak and Prestige. Your Gem reward increases by 1 Gem every 50 Streak.

When is the next update?

Updates take time. There is a Progress channel in the official Discord server, which you can use to get an understanding of what stage of development each of the features are in.

Please do not ask testers, staff, or developers about the update's contents or release date. Any information about the update will be publicly released when it is ready to be shared.

What do Luck Potions boost?

Luck Potions, as well as all other modifiers to the base Luck stat, exclusively boost drop chances for raids and missions. They do NOT affect anything else, such as Family changes or cosmetic crate rates.

How I join a private server?

On the Main Menu:

1. Click on codes

2. In the bottom left, enter or paste the private server's code and press enter

Do Regiments cost Robux?

It costs Robux to create a Regiment, but joining them is free. You just have to be in the Roblox group linked to the Regiment you want to join.

What are codes, and how do I use them?

Codes are created by the developers, and released in the announcements channel of the official Discord server. They last for one week, before expiring forever. You can begin using codes as soon as you are Level 15 from the main menu, by selecting "CODES" and inputting them in the box that appears on the bottom right of your screen.

New codes come out periodically, so be patient.

What is the best Artifact sub-stat?

It depends on your build. Generally, ODM, Shifter, or Crit Damage are good, though Skill Cooldown Reduction can be more powerful in certain applications (such as Mission grinds).

What's the difference between Damage Reduction and Damage Resistance?

Damage Reduction follows the formula Taken_Damage = Damage / (1 + (DAMAGE_REDUCTION / 100)), meaning each point of Damage Reduction becomes less valuable the more of it you have (diminishing returns).

Damage Resistance is actual damage negation, meaning X% Damage Resistance will truly reduce damage taken by X%.
