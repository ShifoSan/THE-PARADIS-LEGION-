⚔️ Titan Combat Briefing: Know Your Shifter ⚔️

Listen up, Legionaries! The Colossal Titan update is upon us. To achieve victory, you must understand the tools of destruction at your disposal. Titans are categorized into two distinct combat doctrines: the versatile Standard Build and the devastating Glass Cannon.

Know their strengths and weaknesses, or you will be devoured.

Part 1: The Standard Build - The Jack-of-All-Trades

Most Shifters you'll encounter—like the Attack, Female, and Armored—are built on a reliable and balanced 7-skill combat kit. Think of them as your frontline brawlers, tough and adaptable enough for any situation.

The Standard Titan Toolkit (7 Core Skills):

  • 💥 High-Damage Nuke: Your go-to single-target obliterator. A straight-line attack perfect for deleting a high-priority enemy.
    Examples: Corckscrew Punch (Attack Titan), Sidekick (Female Titan), Earthbreaker (Armour Titan), Tectonic Quake (Colossal Titan)
  • 🌪️ Area-of-Effect (AoE) Clear: The "get off me" button. Use this to smash and control crowds of smaller titans swarming you.
    Examples: Brutal Stomp (Female Titan), Round Hook Kick (Attack Titan), Seismic Shock (Colossal Titan)
  • 😱 Heal & Stun: Your lifesaver. This ability not only gets you back in the fight by restoring health but also stuns nearby enemies, giving you a crucial opening.
    Examples: Roar (Female Titan), Shriek (Attack Titan), Roar (Armour Titan)
  • 💪 Titan-Slaying Finisher: The ultimate flex. Use other titans as your weapon to cause even more carnage.
    Examples: Grapple Titan Toss (Female Titan), Uppercut (Armored Titan), Titan Toss (Armour Titan)
  • ✨ Self-Buff: Your personal power-up. Pop this to temporarily boost your stats, whether it's hardening for defense or courage for more damage.
  • 💨 Eject: The universal escape hatch.
  • The 7th skill is always a Unique Special Move that defines the Titan's true playstyle.

Part 2: The Colossal Titan - GOD OF DESTRUCTION 🔥

The Colossal Titan throws the standard rulebook into the fire. It is a highly specialized weapon of mass destruction, a true NUKE. It ditches versatility and defense for one thing: MAXIMUM DAMAGE.

The Colossal's Arsenal (Just 4 Skills):

  • Seismic Shock: A massive AoE that burns everything around you.
  • Tectonic Quake: A straight-line attack that erases whatever it touches.
  • Incendiary Volley: Turns other titans into explosive projectiles.
  • Heatwave: A pure damage AoE that stuns but does not heal you.

⚠️ The Price of Power: Key Weaknesses ⚠️

  • 💔 NO SELF-HEAL: This is its greatest weakness. The Heatwave ability offers no recovery. Every point of damage you take is permanent for that shift.
  • 🚫 NO BUFFS: You get no "Hardening" or "Courage." What you see is what you get.
  • 脆 LOW DURABILITY: It has less health and a shorter transformation time. Get in, cause destruction, and get out before you're taken down.

🔥 The Secret Weapon: Full Power Resonance 🔥

  • Burn Aura: Weaker titans die just by touching you. (+AURA)
  • Shifting Nuke & Steam Burn: You create a massive damage explosion when you shift in and a damaging steam aura when you eject.
  • Shockwave (The Ultimate Gamble): Activate a continuous damage aura that melts everything around you... but it drains 4% of your own health per second. Use perks like Lifefeed/Soulfeed to mitigate this, or save it for a final, glorious blaze of destruction.

Conclusion: Choose Your Combat Doctrine

👍 Choose a Standard Build Titan if you want a durable, adaptable brawler who can take a hit, heal up, and stay in the fight for the long haul.

☢️ Choose the Colossal Titan if you have the skill to handle a high-risk, high-reward glass cannon. Your mission is to deal overwhelming damage and disappear before the enemy can react. Mistakes will be punished, but victory will be absolute.

✨ The Legion's Master Guide to Prestige ✨

The Legion's Master Guide to Prestige

Prestige resets certain progress while granting permanent buffs. Use this to plan your ascension.

How to Prestige

Requirement: Reach the maximum level for your current prestige rank. You do NOT need a prestige scroll to prestige.

What you KEEP
  • Titan Serum
  • Current in-game Streak
  • Best Boost selected
What RESETS
  • Level (resets to 1)
  • Skill Tree
  • ODM Grade
Permanent Buffs gained
  • + New memory slot per prestige
  • Choice of permanent boost: +7.5% Luck OR +15% EXP OR +15% Gold
  • +10 gems to mission & raid payouts per prestige
  • +0.005% legendary chance per prestige
  • Higher level caps at each prestige (P1:125, P2:150, P3:175, P4:200)
Gold-to-Gem Conversion Examples
  • P1: 1500 Gold = 1 Gem
  • P2: 1250 Gold = 1 Gem
  • P3: 1000 Gold = 1 Gem
  • P4: 1000 Gold = 1 Gem

Prestiging unlocks trading privileges at higher tiers. For the Legion!

🔰 A Legionary's Guide to Targeted Memory Rerolling

A Legionary's Guide to Targeted Memory Rerolling

This guide teaches a 5-step method to target specific memories by manipulating your skill tree and using reset/scroll resources.

Required Materials
  • 1x Skill Tree Reset Scroll
  • Prestige Scrolls
  • Gold and Gems for resets
The 5-Step Rerolling Doctrine
  1. Reset your skill tree with 999 gems; leave it empty.
  2. Choose the memory category you want (Offense/Defense/Supply).
  3. Invest points in skills of that category only to bias the pool.
  4. Use Prestige Scrolls to reroll memories; you'll see mostly chosen-category results.
  5. Revert to your intended build after you get the memory you want.

Warning: This significantly increases probabilities (up to ~90%). Use resources wisely.

AoTR FAQs

What are all the secret achievements?

Omnipotent - Have all achievements (beside Omnipotent)

Overlord - 50,000 kills

Fearless - 20,000 streak

Champion - Top 3 on monthly leaderboard (for the end of the month)

Immortalized - 500 deaths

Warbringer - Complete any Aberrant raid with the first 8 modifiers. NOTE: You must get at least 25.0% damage credit to receive Warbringer.

Legend - 2,500 missions

Encounter - Play a game with a Developer, Administrator, or Moderator. NOTE: Trello Staff, Trial Moderators, Helpers, and Testers can NOT give Encounter.

How should I upgrade my gear?

The most important stat to upgrade is Damage, as being able to 1-shot titans will both make the game much easier and much faster. Upgrading all stats is important, though, as your overall Grade is based on the average of all of your stats' Grades combined, but the upgrade cost is only based on the Grade of that specific stat. Increasing your overall Grade as quickly and cheaply as possible will let you enter higher difficulties, and thus earn better rewards.

How many achievements can I skip for Shiki?

One. Make it count. This includes secret (red) achievements. For most players, Champion is the best achievement to skip.

When is X2 Family?

Starts every weekend on:

  • Saturday 12:00 A.M. (UTC) - Tuesday 12:00 A.M. (UTC)
  • Friday 7:00 P.M. (EST) - Monday 7:00 P.M. (EST)
  • Saturday 12:00 A.M. (UK) - Tuesday 12:00 A.M. (UK)

The game runs on UTC timezone, just convert to your own timezone if it isn’t listed.

How do Streak Gems work?

On Hard and above, every time you complete a mission where your total streak hits a new multiple of 50, you get Gems based on the mission’s difficulty, as well as your Streak and Prestige. Your Gem reward increases by 1 Gem every 50 Streak.

When is the next update?

Updates take time. There is a Progress channel in the official Discord server, which you can use to get an understanding of what stage of development each of the features are in.

Please do not ask testers, staff, or developers about the update's contents or release date. Any information about the update will be publicly released when it is ready to be shared.

What do Luck Potions boost?

Luck Potions, as well as all other modifiers to the base Luck stat, exclusively boost drop chances for raids and missions. They do NOT affect anything else, such as Family changes or cosmetic crate rates.

How do I join a private server?

On the Main Menu:

  1. Click on Codes
  2. In the bottom left, enter or paste the private server’s code and press enter
Do Regiments cost Robux?

It costs Robux to create a Regiment, but joining them is free. You must be in the Roblox group linked to the Regiment you want to join.

What are codes, and how do I use them?

Codes are created by the developers and released in the announcements channel of the official Discord server. They last for one week before expiring forever. You can begin using codes as soon as you are Level 15 from the main menu, by selecting “CODES” and inputting them in the box that appears on the bottom right of your screen.

New codes come out periodically, so be patient.

What is the best Artifact sub-stat?

It depends on your build. Generally, ODM, Shifter, or Crit Damage are good, though Skill Cooldown Reduction can be more powerful in certain applications (such as Mission grinds).

What’s the difference between Damage Reduction and Damage Resistance?

Damage Reduction follows the formula: Taken_Damage = Damage / (1 + (DAMAGE_REDUCTION / 100)), meaning each point of Damage Reduction becomes less valuable the more of it you have (diminishing returns).

Damage Resistance is actual damage negation, meaning X% Damage Resistance will truly reduce damage taken by X%.
