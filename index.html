3. The Legion's Master Guide to Prestige

Prestige is at the core of AoTR player growth. Maximizing your Prestige gains unlocks powerful abilities and level caps.

  • The Path to Prestige: How to Prestige: Requirement: To prestige, you only need to reach the maximum level for your current prestige rank. Prestige Scrolls: You do NOT need a prestige scroll to prestige. Prestige scrolls are a separate item used only for rerolling memories.
  • The Aftermath: Consequences & Rewards: Prestiging is a monumental step that resets certain aspects of your progress while granting permanent buffs. What You KEEP After Prestiging: Your Titan Serum. Your current in-game Streak. The Best Boost you have selected (e.g., Luck). What RESETS After Prestiging: Your Level resets to 1. Your Skill Tree resets. Your ODM Grade resets. The Permanent Buffs You GAIN: New Memory Slot: You gain an additional passive/permanent memory slot with every prestige. Choice of Permanent Boost: You can choose one of the following permanent stat increases: +7.5% Luck OR +15% EXP OR +15% Gold Increased Gem Payouts: Every prestige adds a permanent +10 gems to your mission and raid payouts. Increased Legendary Chance: You gain a permanent +0.005% chance to roll legendary families per prestige. Increased Level Cap: Your maximum attainable level increases with each prestige: Prestige 1: New Level Cap is 125 Prestige 2: New Level Cap is 150 Prestige 3: New Level Cap is 175 Prestige 4: New Level Cap is 200
  • Converting Your Fortune: Gold-to-Gem Conversion: Upon prestiging, all of your accumulated Gold is automatically converted into Gems. The conversion rate improves as you reach higher prestige levels. Prestiging to P1: 1500 Gold = 1 Gem. Prestiging to P2: 1250 Gold = 1 Gem. Prestiging to P3: 1000 Gold = 1 Gem. Prestiging to P4: 1000 Gold = 1 Gem. Example Conversion (with 500 Million Gold which is the cap on gold-gem conversion): Prestige 1: 333,333 gems. Prestige 2: 400,000 gems. Prestige 3: 500,000 gems. Prestige 4: 500,000 gems.

Mastering Prestige timing and buff management will give you an edge in rank and combat effectiveness.
